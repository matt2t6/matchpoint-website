    Physics Engine

    Fatigue vs. Serve Velocity

    The simulation enforces bio-mechanical reality. As physical fatigue accumulates, serve velocity decays. But pressure points — break points, tiebreaks — inject adrenaline and cause temporary velocity spikes.

    Fatigue penalty: every 5% fatigue rise drops serve speed ~1 km/h
    Adrenaline spikes: break & set points override decay — peak velocity restored
    AO2022 demo data: Nadal velocity climbs from 187 → 200 km/h (116 → 124 mph) as comeback builds
